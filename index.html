Home

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher in AI-assisted electricity market design for sustainability at Technical University of Denmark (DTU) and a recipient of EuroTechPostdoc2 programme fellowship. I am using both optimization and machine learning (Online and Reinforcement Learning) techniques for electricity market problems.

I was a Postdoctoral Research Fellow in Algorithmic trading in energy markets with market microstructure methods at Scuola Normale Superiore di Pisa (Italy).

I received my Ph.D. in Mathematical Methods of Economics, Finance, and Actuarial Sciences, from Brescia University (2021). During my PhD, I had one and half years visiting period at Universidad Carlos III de Madrid (uc3m) in Spain.

Research Interests

My research interest includes almost everything that may lie in the intersection of Economics, Finance, Operations Research, and Machine Learning.

The main mathematical tools that I am working with include:

  • Stochastic programming
  • Distributionally robust optimization
  • Duality and complementarity modeling
  • Equilibrium programs with equilibrium constraints (MPEC)
  • Bilevel Programming
  • GMM Estimation
  • Machine Learning
  • Online and Reinforcement Learning

Topics that have been addressed so far using the above methods:

  • Electricity Market with a generation mix
  • Contract Designs in Electricity Markets
  • Emissions Trading Schemes
  • Demand Response Program
  • Market making strategy using dynamic programming and optimal control
  • No-regret algorithms for electricity trading agents
